# Introduction

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook**. This is an AI-native textbook platform designed to guide you through the exciting world of embodied intelligence.

## Course Overview

This textbook is structured as a 13-week intensive learning journey. Each week, you will explore different aspects of Physical AI, from the fundamentals of embodiment to the complexities of real-world deployment.

## Weekly Breakdown {#weekly-breakdown}

Here is a high-level overview of what we will cover:

- **Week 1**: Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics
- **Week 2**: Actuators and Mechanisms
- **Week 3**: Sensors and Perception
- **Week 4**: Control Systems and Feedback Loops
- **Week 5**: Robot Kinematics and Dynamics
- **Week 6**: Localization and Mapping (SLAM)
- **Week 7**: Motion Planning and Navigation
- **Week 8**: Machine Learning for Robotics
- **Week 9**: Computer Vision and Object Recognition
- **Week 10**: Human-Robot Interaction
- **Week 11**: Sim-to-Real Transfer
- **Week 12**: Ethics and Safety in Robotics
- **Week 13**: Final Project and Future Frontiers

## How to Use This Textbook

- **Read**: Go through each chapter sequentially.
- **Interact**: Use the built-in AI tutor to ask questions about the content.
- **Practice**: Complete the assessment and lab tasks at the end of each chapter.
